Lost Property Posting

Taxi drivers and owners must return property they find in a taxicab. If they cannot return the property directly to you, they need to bring it to a designated police precinct. Remember that after a cab drops you off, usually the next passenger is likely to find any left property at the back seat of the taxi. Filing an anonymous public ad posting on our website will increase your chances in targeting both public audience and yellow cab drivers who may have found your property.
